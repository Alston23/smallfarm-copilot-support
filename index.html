Common Questions

How do I get started?

After signing in, start by adding your fields/beds, then your crops. Once those are set up, you can create plantings and begin tracking inventory, revenue, and tasks.

Does the app work offline?

Some features may work with a temporary connection loss, but SmallFarm Copilot is designed primarily for use with an internet connection so it can sync your data securely.

What’s included in the free version?

Free users can use the core planning and tracking tools, plus ask basic questions in the AI Assistant. Advanced, data-driven recommendations and Pro AI features require a Pro subscription.

What is included in Pro?

  • Advanced crop and planting recommendations based on your farm data
  • Smarter fertilizer and planning suggestions
  • Deeper analytics and AI-powered insights

Billing & Subscriptions

SmallFarm Copilot Pro is a subscription purchased through the App Store. Apple manages billing and payment processing.

  • You can manage or cancel your subscription in your Apple ID account settings.
  • Your subscription will auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the renewal date.
  • Charges are billed to your Apple ID account.

Privacy

SmallFarm Copilot stores your farm data so the app can work correctly and personalize recommendations. We do not sell your personal data.
