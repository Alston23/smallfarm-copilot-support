Privacy Policy

App: SmallFarm Copilot

Last updated: December 11, 2025

Information We Collect

  • Contact Info: email address (if you create an account, sign in, or contact support).
  • User Content: information you enter into the app (e.g., crops, fields/beds, tasks, notes) and any photos you choose to upload.
  • Location (Coarse): approximate location, if you allow it (used for weather and farm planning features).
  • Usage Data: product interaction and basic usage events to improve features and reliability.
  • Diagnostics: crash data and performance data to troubleshoot issues and improve stability.
  • Identifiers: a user ID used to associate app data with your account.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core app functionality (planning, tracking, weather-based insights, syncing your data).
  • Maintain accounts and authenticate sign-in.
  • Improve the app, fix bugs, and monitor performance.
  • Respond to support requests.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help operate the app (e.g., hosting, analytics, crash reporting) only to provide and improve the service.

Location

If you grant permission, we use coarse location to provide location-based features like local weather and related recommendations. You can disable location access in your device settings at any time.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the app and support, and as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data.

Your Choices

  • Update or correct information in the app.
  • Disable permissions (like location) in device settings.
  • Request account/data deletion by contacting support.
